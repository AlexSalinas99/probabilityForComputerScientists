Beta Random Variable

Notation: $X \sim \Beta(\alpha, \beta)$
Description: A belief distribution for an unknown probability. Captures confidence.
Parameters: $\alpha > 0$, number of imagined successes +1
$\beta > 0$, number of imagined failures +1
Support: $x \in [0,1]$
PDF equation: $$f(x) = K \cdot x ^{\alpha - 1} \cdot (1-x)^{\beta - 1}$$
Expectation: $\E[X] = \frac{\alpha}{\alpha+\beta}$
Mode: $\text{Mode}(X) = \frac{\alpha - 1}{\alpha+\beta - 2}$
Variance: $\var(X) = \frac{\alpha\beta}{(\alpha + \beta)^2 (\alpha + \beta + 1)}$
