Expectation

Definition: Expectation

$$ \E[X] = \sum_x \p(X=x) \cdot x $$

The full picture of a random variable is given by its likelihood function: either a PMF if it is discrete or, as we will see in a few chapters a PDF if it is continuous.

Intuition of expectation

Properties of expectation

Property: Linearity of Expectation

$$E[aX + b] = a\E[X]+b$$

Property: Expectation of the Sum of Random Variables

$$E[X+Y] = E[X] +E[Y]$$

Property: Expectation of a Constant

$$E[a] = a$$

Property: Law of Unconcious Statistician

$$E[g(X)] = \sum_x g(x)\p(X=x)$$
